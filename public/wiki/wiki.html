How to Play

Overview

Sorcer.io is a game about sorcery, and each player in the game is a wizard. Your goal as a wizard is to kill the other wizards and collect the most points/xp, leveling up and unlocking new spells along the way. You achieve this by casting spells on your opponents. You can collect many different spells, each with a their own effect. These effects all do very different things. For example, the "blind spell" will "blind" an opponent, causing their screen to go dark . Each time that you level up, you will be given a choice of spells, and you can choose one to unlock. Higher levels will unlock more powerful spells than lower levels.

Game Interface

  • Leaderboard: The leaderboard displays the top 10 players and their scores. If you make it onto the leaderboard, your nickname will be highlighed in green.
  • Your player: Your player will always be green, and will always stay centered in the middle of the screen.
  • Enemy player: Enemy players will show up red, and their nickname and level will be displayed underneath them. Note: Your own nickname and level do not show on your screen, but they do appear on opponent's screens.
  • XP bar: The XP bar is the smaller of the 2 bars, with a green slider. It shows your progress towards the next level.
  • Health bar: The Health bar is larger than the xp bar, and it has a grey slider. It displays how much health you have remaining.
  • Your stats: Your stats show how much XP you have, what your level is, and what percentage your health is at.
  • Your spells/hotbar: The hotbar displays 4 "quick access" spells of your choosing. The spell that is currently selected will be outlined in yellow. Other spells that you have unlocked are saved in your inventory.

Movement

Use the WASD keys to move up, left, down, and right, respectively. Move your mouse around the screen to aim. The edges of the game are red, and you cannot move any farther past them. This is what the game border looks like:

Casting Spells

To cast a spell, click anywhere on the screen. This will cast your selected spell, aimed in the direction of your mouse cursor. When you cast a spell, it's cooldown will be triggered. Once a spell is casted, it cannot be casted again until it has finished cooling down. While a spell is cooling down, it will look like this in your inventory:

Once the spell has fully regained it's color, and there is no more grey showing, the spell has cooled down and is able to be casted again.

Spells

Types of Spells

There are two main types of spells in the game: projectile spells, and splash spells.

Projectile Spells

Projectile spells are the most basic type of spell. They behave much like a bullet fired from a gun: They travel at a certain speed, for a certain distance. If they hit a player along the way, the player will be affected with that spell's effect. If a projectile spell reaches it's target without hitting a player, it will dissapear and no effects will be dealt.

Splash Spells

Splash Spells behave very similarly to projectile spells, until a certain point. After they reach their target, they explode, creating an "effect area" for a certain amount of time. Any players inside the effect area will be affected with the splash spell's effect. If a splash spell hits a player before it reaches it's target, it will explode immediately. Fire Spell


  • Type: Projectile
  • Speed: 1
  • Range: 700
  • Effect: Affected player gets -100 health
  • Cooldown: 0.7 seconds

Freeze Spell


  • Type: Projectile
  • Speed: 0.7
  • Range: 500
  • Effect: Affected player gets "frozen" and cannot move for 3 seconds
  • Cooldown: 5 seconds

Blind Spell


  • Type: Splash

  • Range: 700
